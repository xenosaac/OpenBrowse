Design Principles (all options share these)

Emerald Accent
#10b981 as primary, used for active states, secure indicators, interactive highlights
No Cheap Gradients

Micro-interactions
Buttons feel physical: subtle scale on press, emerald glow on hover, smooth 200ms transitions
SF Pro + Precision
Consistent 6/8px radius, tight spacing, SF Pro Display typography, macOS-native feel
