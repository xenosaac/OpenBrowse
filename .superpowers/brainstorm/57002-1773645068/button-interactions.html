Button & Interactive Element Design

How should buttons and interactive elements feel? Try hovering and clicking the demos below.

Navigation Buttons (Back, Forward, Reload, Home)

Style A: Emerald Glow on Hover
Hover: emerald glow halo + border tint. Press: scale down + deeper shadow.
Style B: Glass Reveal on Hover
Invisible at rest. Hover: glass panel materializes. Press: depress effect. Minimal until interaction.

Primary Action Buttons (Send, Submit, CTA)

Style A: Solid Emerald + Press Depth
Solid fill. Hover: lift + glow spread. Press: push in with inset shadow. Feels physical.
Style B: Glass Emerald + Shimmer
Glass fill with emerald tint. Hover: shimmer sweep + glow. Press: scale compress. Feels ethereal.
